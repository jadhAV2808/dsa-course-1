
Queue: FIFO

operations: 
	1] enqueue()
	2] dequeue()
	3] getFront()
	4] getRear()
	5] size()
	6] isEmpty()

Applications of Queue: 
1]  single resource & multiple consumers
2] synchronization between slow & fast devices
3] in operating systems(semaphores, FCFS scheduling)
4] in computer n/w (routers/switches and mail queues)
