Intro: 
for every node, data in left node are smaller & 
data in right side is greater.
all keys are typically considered as distinct.
linked DS.

Applications of BST: 
 to maintain sorted stream of data(or sorted set of data)
 to implement doubly ended priority Queue.
 to solve problems like: 
    1] count  smaller/greater in a stream.
    2] floor/ ceiling/greater/smaller in a stream.
